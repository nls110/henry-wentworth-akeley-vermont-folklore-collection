## About the Collection

Dr. Henry Wentworth Akeley was a folklorist active in the early 20th century who focused on documenting the regional traditions, oral histories, and cultural practices of the people of Vermont.  Dr. Akeley traveled accross the state between 1902 through 1931 to record local stories to create manuscripts, maps and bound volumes.
